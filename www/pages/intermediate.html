What intermediates should know

Intermediate level students at American Karate Studios take on new responsibilities, such as competition and weaponry. A wide variety of competitive options are available to students, as well as training in bo staff, nunchaku, and various swords. Also, American Karate Studios is involved in the arts of competitive breaking, musical forms, and kick-boxing.
