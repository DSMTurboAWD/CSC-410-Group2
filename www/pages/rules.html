Club Rules
Golden Rule

Do onto other as you would have them do onto you.

Rule 1

Treat your instructor and others with respect


With the exception of yells and responsive items, please keep the chatter down. The instructor must be able to gather the attention of the entire class at any time, with minimal effort. Additionally, please make sure you treat your fellow students with respect.

Rule 2

Only hit your opponent as hard as you would like to be hit


Often times during training, you will be sparing, in those instances, you may make contact with other students However, all combat should be light touch. If you do strike another student with more force, expect to be reciprocated

Rule 3

Enjoy yourself, have a good workout


Martial Arts is high energy, high cardio sport. As such you will sweat, you get a workout. Have fun and enjoy yourself
